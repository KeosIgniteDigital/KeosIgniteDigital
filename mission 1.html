" THE EMDR "

How to use this tool ?

>> Pick an issue that bother you <<


-On a scale of 0 to 10, where 0 is no disturbance and 10 is the highest disturbance imaginable, how disturbing does it feel to you now?





-bring up that incident and those words (negative cognition), what emotions do you feel now?”


– “Where do you feel that in your body?


-bring up that picture, those negative words (repeat the negative cognition), notice where you are feeling it in your body, and follow the movment of the "EYES" above !
